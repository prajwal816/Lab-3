Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find the answer to your question below:

Account & Billing

How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I change my subscription plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account settings.

Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings or by contacting support.

Courses & Learning

How long do I have access to courses?
Paid subscribers have unlimited access to all courses as long as their subscription is active.

Can I download course videos?
Yes, Pro and Enterprise subscribers can download videos for offline viewing.

Do you offer certificates?
Yes, all courses include completion certificates that you can share on LinkedIn.

Is there a mobile app?
Yes, our mobile apps are available for iOS and Android devices.
